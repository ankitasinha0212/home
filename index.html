    About R.N Inter College

    R.N Inter College was founded in 1937 .It was the first institution of learning opened to cater to the educational needs of students in Parsagarh Saran. This college is affiliated by Bihar School Examination Board, Patna.

    		  
    Our Vision
    Rooted in the life, vision, and teachings of God and inspired by Dr. Radha Krishnan Das, the foundress of the Apostolic Carmel, R.N Inter College Parsagarh Saran strives to become a center of academic excellence in higher education, social responsibility, and providing education to needy.
     
    Our Mission
    R.N Inter College Parsagarh Saran, the first institute in Parsagarh for student, is committed to the holistic development of students so as to make an effective contribution to the creation of a better society.
     
    Our Core Value
    • Faith in God
    • Honesty /Moral Uprightness
    • Love and Forgiveness towards all
    • Social Responsibility/empowerment of the Marginalized
    • Respect for Life and Creation
